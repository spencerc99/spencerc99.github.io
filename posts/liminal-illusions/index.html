Liminal Illusions

exploring the liminality of vegas and how it exposes the absurdity of our day-to-day

Every time I visit Vegas, I hope its caricature will be a sobering reminder of this belief, that we can escape the liminality and reach that utopian destination we hope for, even if we have to crawl one foot at a time to get there.
