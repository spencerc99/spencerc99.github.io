Who am I?

I'm a programmer, artist, and researcher who creates infrastructure (tools, open-source libraries, instruction manuals) and internet environments (living ecosystems, playful spaces, and games) that highlight the humanity shining in every seam of the computers we use and the materiality of these invisible bits and pixels. I want to create space for real dialogue between the human and the computer, where computing is a medium for people to express themselves, cherish others, and create the possibility for imaginative change.

🆕 Open to collaborations for provocative internet experiences, speculative infrastructure for a defensibly open internet, and community-embedded computing (bio, art overview)
