Dissonance,
uninhibited heights
cooled by night

Whispers
urgently pressed
to reconcile minds

Outbursts
Melodic giggles
chasing timeless moments

Silences
Tension dripping
Pillows smothered down

Two-toned
Tense light
tale of life.
