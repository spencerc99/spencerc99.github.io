Coda Packs Platform

a platform for extending Coda’s functionality by bringing in and taking action on external apps' data

  • Created Pack Cards, a live data, customizable card for external app data.
  • Created OpenAI Pack, an integration for everyone to leverage AI for their work and creative expression with their everyday tools. #6 on ProductHunt.
